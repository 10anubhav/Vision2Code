ABOUT US

Welcome to Vision2Code, an innovative platform that transforms hand-drawn UI sketches into functional HTML code with ease. Our mission is to bridge the gap between design and development, enabling users to quickly convert their ideas into working code.

Vision2Code leverages advanced AI algorithms to recognize UI elements from scanned sketches and generate accurate HTML code, complete with a live preview. Whether you're a designer looking to prototype ideas or a developer aiming to speed up your workflow, our platform provides an intuitive and efficient solution.

With Vision2Code, users can simply upload a hand-drawn UI sketch, and within seconds, receive a structured HTML output along with a real-time preview of their design. The generated code can be further customized and integrated into any web project. Designed for simplicity, accuracy, and efficiency, Vision2Code empowers individuals, students, and professionals to bring their UI concepts to life effortlessly.
